Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) are metrics that measure the success of a product or service based on specific goals and objectives. KPIs help businesses track their progress toward achieving their targets and identify areas that require improvement.

## When to use

KPIs are used throughout the product development lifecycle to measure the success of a product or service against predetermined goals and objectives. They are useful for identifying areas that require improvement and making data-driven decisions.

<div class="mt-8 mb-8 grid grid-cols-2 divide-x overflow-hidden rounded-md border">
  <div class="py-6 px-10 space-y-4">
    **Pros**

    - KPIs provide a quantitative and objective measure of the success of a product or service.
    - They help businesses track their progress toward achieving their targets and goals.
    - KPIs are useful for identifying areas that require improvement and making data-driven decisions.
    - They can be easily communicated to stakeholders and team members.

  </div>
  <div class="py-6 px-10 space-y-4">
    **Cons**

    - KPIs can be limited in their ability to provide insights into the underlying reasons for success or failure.
    - They may not be able to capture the subjective experiences and emotions of users.
    - The selection of KPIs may be subjective and depend on the goals and objectives of the business.

  </div>
</div>
